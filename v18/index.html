Vidal & Osei Advance to Miami Semifinals as Volkov Awaits

Alejandro Vidal dispatched Yuki Tanaka in straight sets — 7–5, 6–3 — on Tuesday at Hard Rock Stadium, propelling the third-ranked Spaniard into a semifinal collision with Marcus Osei, who outlasted Rafael Montoya in a gruelling three-set affair that stretched beyond two hours. All eyes now turn to Thursday's quarterfinal, where Dmitri Volkov faces Noah Eriksen in what promises to be a clash of contrasting styles on the Miami hard courts.
